Support for Kiwi TCMS

Public Tenant users or anyone not on a commercial subscription plan:

  • Use GitHub Issue Tracker to file bugs and feature requests
  • Ask questions with "kiwi-tcms" tag on Stack Overflow
  • Priority rule: as-we-see-fit

Do not ask individual project members for support, your request will be ignored! Use the channels above instead, where the whole community can help you and benefit from the solutions provided. If community support is insufficient for your situation, consider commercial support!

Private Tenant users or customers on a commercial subscription plan from MrSenko:

  • Email: kiwitcms-@-mrsenko.com
  • Working hours: 09-17 UTC/Mon-Fri
  • Priority rule: see MrSenko.com
  • Requests must include your email/GitHub username used in the subscription
